About Me

Jasmin Moghbeli

Jasmin Moghbeli was selected by NASA to join the 2017 Astronaut Candidate Class. She reported for duty in August 2017 and having completed the initial astronaut candidate training is now eligible for a mission assignment. The New York native earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Aerospace Engineering with Information Technology at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and a Master of Science in Aerospace Engineering from the Naval Postgraduate School. Moghbeli, an AH-1W Super Cobra pilot and Marine Corps test pilot, has over 150 combat missions and 2,000 hours of flight time in over 25 different aircraft.

August 3rd, 2017 03:27

It's launch day! The rest of my cohort and myself have been training for this day for what feels like years. We're on our way to build the first colony on Mars, something we've been striving for forever. It's here! The day is finally here!

My crewmates and I have spend the past few weeks getting to know each other, especially seeing as it's just the 7 of us stranded here in space until the colonists arrive. There have been bonding activities, games, socials, and everything in between to get to know each other well. It'll be a year of strenous activity building the structures for the colonists to arrive in. We won't have a lot of downtime or bonding time, so it'll require a lot of trust. I think we've gotten there! There might be one or two of my crew I don't rely on as much, but for the most part I know we're all here because we're invested in the future!
